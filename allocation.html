Allocation Rules

Keep machine movement clear for production, maintenance, and vendor follow-up.

New Allocation Use when a machine is first placed in a line after receive or store hold. Line Transfer Use when the machine shifts to another line but stays in active production. Store Hold Use when the machine is waiting for PM, service, return, or next deployment decision. Vendor Check Rent machines may need vendor or agreement review before transfer or replacement use.
Allocation history should keep from location, to location, date, and reason clear. This makes future backend tracking, machine history, and reports much easier.
