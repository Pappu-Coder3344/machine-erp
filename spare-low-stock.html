Operational Notes

Keep low stock review practical for store and maintenance coordination.

  • Machine dependency helps decide whether a shortage affects PM, breakdown, or both workflows.
  • Purchase follow-up can later use the same part codes and supplier names without page redesign.
  • Reports and dashboard should be able to pull low stock risk directly from this structure later.
